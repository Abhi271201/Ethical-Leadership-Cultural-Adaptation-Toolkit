🗣️ Leadership Communication Style Statement

My leadership communication style is built on transparency, active listening, and empowering team members. I believe in clear, concise messaging that fosters trust and inclusivity. I adapt my tone and delivery based on context—whether it's motivating the team during a high-stakes project or facilitating open dialogue during feedback sessions. My goal is to create a psychologically safe environment where every voice matters.

🎯 Performance Coaching Script

Purpose: To guide a one-on-one performance coaching session effectively.

🔹 Start with Positives:
"Thank you for your hard work on the last sprint. Your problem-solving during the UI bug fix was excellent."

🔹 Clarify Area for Growth:
"I noticed some delays in updating the Jira tickets. Can we discuss what might be getting in the way?"

🔹 Explore Solutions Collaboratively:
"Would a daily 5-minute sync help? Or do you prefer another system of accountability?"

🔹 Set Goals:
"Let’s aim to complete task updates within 24 hours for the next two weeks."

🔹 Close with Encouragement:
"I believe in your ability to improve this. Let's reconnect next week to see how it's going."
